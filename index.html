About Me

First thing's first, my name is Chad Lew and this is my personal website. Well, I love coding and messing around with CSS and Javascript mostly, practicing on websites like edabit.com and mostly reading documentation from Mozilla and w3schools. I first found about coding when I was a freshman in college, and it appealed a lot to me considering how much time I normally spend on the computer. However as life struck its hardest course, I had to take a detour and I majored in Sociology (totally different). This doesn't surprise me because I chose something that was a little less practical and more easy (at least for me). This would ultimately turn out actually better than expected though. As I look back at my sociology major whilst currently in Coding Bootcamp, I try and connect the dots and see how they both can work together. Even if they don't, I really still enjoying coding because it's hands-on computer related. I've always been fascinated by technology and what can do to people. I started off with an old white iMac I believe it was around 2007 if I am not mistaken. That thing was a tank, that would be the start of my journey of becoming "computer literate". There I learned how to type, edit, how to upload and download, basic navigation controls, terminal, ports, Wi-Fi issues/connections, and everything else we can think of doing on the computer as a 12 year old kid. Granted I wasn't the best at this stuff, but that iMac taught me everything I needed to learn as a kid to self-sufficient as an adult.
